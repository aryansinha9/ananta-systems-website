Privacy Policy for AI Inbox

Last Updated: July 11, 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to AI Inbox ("the App," "we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect information in relation to our services.

Our application provides a service ("the Service") that allows business owners ("Clients," "you") to connect their Instagram Business Account to an AI-powered assistant. This assistant helps manage and respond to Instagram Direct Messages sent by their customers ("End-Users").

This policy applies to Clients who sign up for the AI Inbox service and End-Users who interact with a business using our Service.

2. Information We Collect

A. Information from Clients (Business Users)

When you sign up for AI Inbox using Facebook Login, we collect:

  • Account Information: Your name, email address, and public profile picture provided by Meta.
  • Business Asset Information: A list of your Facebook Pages, the linked Instagram Business Account ID and username, and a Page Access Token for the page you connect.
  • Configuration Data: The Google Sheet ID you provide to configure your AI's knowledge base.

B. Information from End-Users (Customers of our Clients)

When you send a message to a business using our Service, we process:

  • Message Content: The text of your Direct Messages.
  • Basic Profile Information: Your Instagram Scoped User ID (IGSID), public username, and profile picture provided by Meta.
  • Metadata: Timestamps of messages.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used exclusively to provide, maintain, and improve the AI Inbox service.

  • For Clients: To create your account, manage your service, connect to your Instagram page, and enable the AI to respond on your behalf using your business data.
  • For End-Users: To generate an AI response to your message, route the reply correctly, and display the conversation in the business owner's private dashboard for quality assurance.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. Data is shared only with essential third-party service providers required for the App to function:

  • Meta (Facebook/Instagram): To send and receive messages via their API.
  • OpenAI: To process message text to generate AI responses.
  • Google: To access the data in the Google Sheet you provide.
  • Cloud Hosting Providers (Vercel, Render, Railway): To host our application and store data securely.
  • Database Provider (MongoDB Atlas): To securely store all user and conversation data.

5. Data Security and Retention

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information. We retain Client data as long as you use our service and retain End-User conversation data for a period necessary for our Clients to provide customer service.

6. Your Data Rights and Choices

You may contact us to inquire about your data. Clients can disconnect the service via their Facebook Business Integration settings. End-Users can manage their data by deleting messages in their Instagram chat.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect their information.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy at this URL and updating the "Last Updated" date.
